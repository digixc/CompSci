Welcome to Year 5 Computing at Wellington College International Shanghai

Curriculum Outlines

Unit 0: Office Talks

    In this unit, you will develop your understanding of how to use Microsoft Office 365 effectively in all curriculum areas:

  • Avatars
  • Teams
  • OneNote
  • OneDrive
  • Outlook

Unit 1: Tinkering with TinkerCAD

    Building off the soft skills students learnt in Year 4, students will create a 3D model related to their topic that they will 3d Print

  • Reminder of how to use TinkerCAD
  • Creating a Mayan Temple
  • Custom challenges

Unit 2: Lies, damned lies, and fake news

    In this unit, students will learn how to research effectively, discover how to see fake news, and produce thier own fake news article

  • Research skills
  • How to tell if something is fake
  • Editing fake images
  • Producing a fake article using that fake image

Unit 3: Go do Kodu

    In this unit, students will create a game using the Kodu software

  • Discovering what makes a good game
  • Designing and creating a world
  • Coding your game
  • Playtesting each others games

Unit 4: DJ: Sonic Pi

    In this unit, students will discover the relationship between coding and music, seeing how they are algorithmically linked

  • Sonic Pi basics
  • How to code a simple nursary rhyme
  • Code your own creation
  • Sonic Pi music challenges

Unit 5: Robot domination

    In this unit, students will learn how to connect and control robots via a bluetooth network

  • What is bluetooth?
  • Lego WeDo
  • MBot robots
