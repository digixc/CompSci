Welcome to Year 7 Digital Media at Wellington College International Shanghai

Robotics&Physical Computing Progression Pathway

Unit 0: Office Talks

    In this unit, you will develop your understanding of Microsoft Office 365 applications and how to use them effectively in every curriculum area:

  • Getting Around MS Teams for Good
  • Making the Most of OneNote
  • Effective Using OneDrive
  • More Than Just Email: Outlook

Unit 1: You've been framed!

    Your first creative unit of work will focus on digital photography. You will be introduced to important composition guidelines, learn simple editing skills and graphic design techniques

  • Digital Photography
  • Rule of thirds
  • Editing apps - Lightroom, Snapseed
  • Graphic Design - Canva

Unit 2: The Evolution of Technology

    In this unit, you will delve into the world of historical technology and look at how technology has evolved over the year. You will develop your presentation skills by creating a detailed, informative Powerpoint to display your independent work.

  • Technology and Music
  • Communication
  • Storage
  • Technology and Game Characters

Unit 3: STOP! Motion Time

    In this detailed unit, you will learn about different types of stop motion animation and research a famous Claymation film. You will develop your graphic design skills and then learn tips to create your own Claymation animations which you will then edit to create a final video complete with sound effects and backing music.

  • Independent Research
  • Claymation Infographics
  • Stop Motion Animations
  • Video Editing with iMovie

Unit 4: DigiArt

    This digital art unit will introduce you to the theory of colour, getting you to think about how colour palettes work together to create interesting art. You will learn about different image types and then create your own work using your iPad and the computers.

  • Colour Theory
  • Bitmaop and Vector Images
  • Creating and linking multiple webpages
  • Procreate
  • Krita

Unit 5: Cause for Applause

    The final unit of the year is a group work project. You will come up with a group name, create a logo (thinking carefully about your colour palette). You will then pick two forms of advertising media to promote a sustainable, eco-friendly product that you have invented.

  • Sustainability
  • Media advertising
  • Pros and Cons of different types of advertising media
