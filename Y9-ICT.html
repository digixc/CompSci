Options Guide

Unit 0: Skill Building for a Better Leaner

    An introduction unit for developing students to be a better learner by equiping them with the right skills:

  • Research
  • Time Management
  • Information Synthesis
  • Presentation

Unit 1: power of Data

    Digital world is filled with data. Social media apps collect data about our posts and our likes Online stores collect data about the products we view:

  • Data entry and data Organisation
  • Data Analysis
  • Impact of the big data
  • Qualitative and quantitative data

Unit 2: Graphic Design

    Producing a digital graphic for a for a specific client:

  • Purpose and properties of digital graphics
  • Plan the creation of a digital graphic
  • Create a digital graphic product
  • Review a digital graphic

Unit 3: Making your Movie

    In this unit you will plan and build your very own movie

  • Capture the scenes onto your computer for editing
  • Learn how to import media into Movie
  • Add a sound file (voiceover) to a segment of the movie
  • to understand the import and transition functions within movie making software.

Unit 4: Understanding computer systems.

    This unit will enable learners to develop ICT skills that will equip them to operate effectively in a business environment

  • techniques to search for, store and share information
  • use software to handle data
  • software to communicate information for a business purpose
  • use software tools to format information
  • modify databases to meet user requirements
  • create user interfaces for databases to meet user requirements
  • analyse a databases suitability for a business purpose
