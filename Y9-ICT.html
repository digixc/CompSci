Unit 0: Skill Building for a Better Leaner

    An introduction unit for developing students to be a better learner by equiping them with the right skills:

  • Research
  • Time Management
  • Information Synthesis
  • Presentation

Unit 1: Data and Information

    In this unit, learners will develop understanding of the digital world from the data perspective, such as social media data and online shopping data. They will learn

  • data entry and data organisation
  • data analysis to get meaningful information
  • impact of the big data
  • social and ethic implications of data

Unit 2: Graphic Design

    In this unit, learners will develop understanding of graphics design and skills in creating compelling graphics for a given purpose. They will learn:

  • purposes and properties of digital graphics
  • how to gather requirements from a client
  • how to translate requirements to storyboards
  • how to use tools to create digital graphics fit for purposes
  • how to critically review digital graphics

Unit 3: Movie Making

    In this unit learners will plan and create their own movies that are fit for a purpose. They will learn

  • how to plan a storyboard
  • how to use devices effectively to capture scenes
  • how to import media into a computer for editing
  • how to add sound tracks or voiceover to a segment of a movie
  • how to use various transitions and effects in a movie editing software.

Unit 4: Data Store and Processing

    This unit will develop learners' understanding of the role of database in the business world. They will learn

  • database principles
  • analyse a databases suitability for a business purpose
  • the basics in database design
  • techniques to search for, store and update data
  • create user interfaces for databases to meet user requirements
