Unit 0: Office Talks

    In this unit, you will develop your understanding of Microsoft Office 365 applications and how to use them effectively in every curriculum area:

  • Getting Around MS Teams for Good
  • Making the Most of OneNote
  • Effective Using OneDrive
  • More Than Just Email: Outlook

Unit 1: Business Introduction

    You have just set up your own graphic design business! You will decide the main focus of this business, based on a curriculum area of your own choice (for example PE: football, or The Arts: dance).

  • What is a graphic designer?
  • Curriculum Area
  • Defined Focus

Unit 2: Building Your Brand

    The next step for your business is to create products to advertise it. You will learn how moodboards can inspire you at the start of a project, create a colour palette and design various marketing materials.

  • What is branding?
  • Powerpoint Template
  • Postcards
  • Logos
  • Colour palettes
  • Moodboards

Unit 3: Digital Photography

    Now you have some advertising materials, it is time to create some actual products to sell. You will start by learning important digital photography skills and take a series of photographs for your business.

Unit 4: Desktop Publishing

    Once you have edited your photographs, you will use them to create a series of informative posters, leaflets and infographics about your focus area.

  • Canva as a Tool
  • Posters
  • Leaflets
  • Infographics

Unit 5: Digital Art and animations

    Graphic designers love to communicate via interesting animations. Your next product to create is a digital animation. In this unit, you will develop your understanding of digital art applications by creating animations from simple drawings.

  • Procreate
  • Krita

Unit 6: Stop Motion Animation

    The next animation you will create with be a Stop motion animation. You will pick a type of your choice and learn how to plan effectively using a storyboard. You will also be introduced to green screen and how this might improve the final quality of your work.

  • Storyboard planning
  • Tips and tricks
  • Greenscreen
  • Editing in iMovie

Unit 7: Presentations

    Your job as a graphic designer is coming to a close. It is time to collate all your work to present it to your boss! In this final unit, you will create a professional, detailed presentation showcasing all your work for the entire year.

  • Powerpoint presentations
  • Kiosk mode
  • Action buttons and hyperlinks
  • Automatic animations and transitions
